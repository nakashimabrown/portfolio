hugo nakashima-brown (he/him)

download cv (pdf)

hugo nakashima-brown is an award-winning designer, woodworker, and teaching artist based in boston, ma. he holds a bfa in painting from rhode island school of design and a degree in cabinet & furniture making from the north bennet street school.

trained in 17th- to 19th-century woodworking traditions, nakashima-brown draws on historical precedents to create furniture balancing timeless aesthetics with contemporary use.

his work has been exhibited nationally and internationally and featured in monocle, architectural digest, and fine woodworking.
