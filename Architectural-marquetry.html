Architectural Marquetry
Solo project
2025
Designed and built by
Hugo Nakashima-Brown
This architectural door panel was made for a client's home in Austin, TX built on a former brownfield above the Colorado River.
Inspired by the “edgeland” landscape surrounding the home and the architecture of the library/guest house where they are installed, the designs, taken from client Christopher Brown's photography in the floodplain, explore ecological paradise in a light industrial brownfield.
←
This liminal "edgeland" space between urban and rural is a habitat not just for wildlife but those living out of governed societal systems.
↓
Fabricating and laminating marquetry at this scale posed some real technical challenges, not only because of accuracy of cutting, but of shrinkage factor when introducing water during the glue up.
